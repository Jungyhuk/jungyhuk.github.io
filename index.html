Xinyun Chen (陈 昕昀)

Email: xinyun.chen at berkeley dot edu

 

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Dawn Song. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from ACM Honored Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I was a research intern at Google Brain, working with Denny Zhou and Chen Liang; a research intern at Facebook AI Research (FAIR), working with Yuandong Tian; a research intern at UC Berkeley, working with Prof. Dawn Song; and a visiting student at National Institute of Informatics, working with Prof. Helmut Prendinger.

My research lies at the intersection of deep learning, programming language, and security. Currently, I mainly focus on neural program synthesis and adversarial machine learning.
